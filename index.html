Our Story / The Villa

Our Beginning

In 2012, the vision for Maison Minh was born when founder Minh Nguyen discovered a historic colonial building in the heart of Saigon. Inspired by the blend of French and Vietnamese architectural elements, he embarked on a journey to create a boutique hotel that would honor the city's rich cultural heritage.

Restoration

The year 2013 marked the beginning of an extensive restoration project. Working with local artisans and international designers, we carefully preserved the building's original features while incorporating modern amenities. Every detail, from hand-carved woodwork to custom-made tiles, was thoughtfully considered.

Design Evolution

Throughout 2014, our design team worked tirelessly to create interiors that would reflect Vietnam's rich cultural tapestry. Drawing inspiration from both colonial-era aesthetics and contemporary Vietnamese art, we developed a unique design language that would become the signature style of Maison Minh.

Final Touches

In 2015, as the physical restoration neared completion, we focused on the finer details that would set Maison Minh apart. From curating a collection of local artwork to developing signature scents and sounds for each space, we ensured that every sensory experience would be memorable for our guests.

Grand Opening

Maison Minh officially opened its doors in 2016, welcoming guests from around the world. Our grand opening celebration honored both the building's storied past and its bright future as a sanctuary of luxury and cultural appreciation in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City.
